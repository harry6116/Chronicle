LITTLE NEMO in SLUMBERLAND

Panel 1

King Morpheus: Why did you not come at once? His Majesty has been waiting for you.

[Image Description: A title banner for the comic. In the center, King Morpheus, a large bearded man in a purple and gold robe, sits on his throne. He is flanked by a guard in golden armor and a princess in a white gown.]

Panel 2

Flip: Oh, I've got to take a little stretch now and then.

[Image Description: A long panel showing a grand hall. On the left, a character in a green outfit and a top hat (Flip) is stretching Nemo's arms up high. In the center, Nemo, in his white nightgown, walks towards a pair of massive, ornate, closed golden gates.]

Panel 3

Guard: Where's your ticket? You'll have to get one at the box office. This is the stage entrance you have come to.

[Image Description: A stout guard in a blue and yellow diamond-patterned tunic and a red cap speaks to Flip and Nemo, who have arrived at the base of a grand staircase.]

Panel 4

Flip: That's right, Nemo. You can't see the princess until we have seen this show. Follow me!

[Image Description: Flip, with his green outfit and top hat, talks to Nemo. A small character in a red and yellow jester-like outfit stands next to Nemo, looking up at Flip.]

Panel 5

Shouting Man: What's the trouble here? Don't you know you are not allowed to make a noise? You will wake him up!

Sign: DON'T WAKE UP

[Image Description: A man with a very large head, wearing a dark suit, leans over a counter and shouts angrily at Nemo. A sign on the counter reads "DON'T WAKE UP".]

Panel 6

Striped Character: Oh! That's all right he is with us. We want front seats for the three of us. See?

[Image Description: A character in a red-and-white striped shirt and a clown-like character with a ruff collar are talking. Nemo stands between them.]

Panel 7

Small Character: I say Nemo isn't this great?

Nemo: It's grand. What is it all about?

Top Hat Character: We are just in time to see the parade of the royal guards.

[Image Description: A parade of guards riding zebras enters from the left. A small character in a plaid skirt watches from the side. Nemo and a short, stout character in a large yellow top hat stand at the bottom of a grand staircase, watching the procession.]

Panel 8

Guard: Little Nemo, we're all so glad to see you. We are honoring you with this parade.

Nemo (thinking): My! This is grand!

[Image Description: The parade continues. Guards on zebras hold up yellow banners that spell out "L E N E M O". Nemo stands on the stairs as one of the guards bows deeply to him. A character in an ornate outfit stands on a balcony watching.]

Panel 9

Character on Balcony: He is the guest of honor, don't you see?

Top Hat Character: Get out of the way there, let us pass!

[Image Description: A wide, dynamic panel showing the parade of guards on striped zebras charging forward. The stout character in the yellow top hat is also riding a small animal, clearing a path.]

Panel 10

Father: So, that's what made you yell! You fell out of bed. Now you get right back into bed and go to sleep and stop that noise. Do you hear me?

[Image Description: The final panel. Nemo has woken up in his bedroom. He is sitting on a white chamber pot next to his bed. His father, in a nightshirt, stands over him with a stern expression.]
